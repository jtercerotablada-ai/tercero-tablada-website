What We Do

Our Services

Since we began, we have been at the forefront of all the latest techniques and technologies, offering a wide variety of services to handle the needs of our clients.

Pre-Design, Value Engineering & Costs

Early-stage structural consulting to optimize design, reduce costs, and align engineering decisions with the project budget from day one.

Structural Engineering with BIM LOD 300

Complete structural design integrated with BIM at Level of Development 300 for precise coordination with architecture and MEP teams.

Structural Review & Optimization

Independent peer review and optimization of existing structural designs to improve efficiency, reduce material usage, and ensure code compliance.

Post-Tensioned Concrete Design, Supply & Stressing

Full-service post-tension engineering: design of slabs and beams, tendon supply, and on-site stressing for efficient long-span structures.

BIM Engineering Development LOD 300

Advanced 3D structural modeling with precise geometry, quantities, and specifications for seamless coordination and construction documentation.

Digital Construction

Leveraging digital tools and workflows to streamline construction processes, reduce errors, and improve project delivery timelines.

Work Team Coordination (BIM)

Managing multidisciplinary teams through BIM collaboration to ensure all trades are aligned and conflicts are resolved before construction.

Clash Detection & Interference Analysis

Identifying and resolving spatial conflicts between structural, mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems before they reach the field.

BIM Direction & Audit

Strategic BIM management, quality auditing, and standards enforcement to ensure models meet project requirements and industry best practices.
